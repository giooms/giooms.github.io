Current Research — Decision Support Algorithms for Optimal Aggregation and Participation in Electricity Markets

As part of my PhD at UCLouvain (LIDAM/CORE), I am working at the intersection of power systems, operations research, and machine learning to develop decision support algorithms for optimal aggregation and participation in electricity markets to relieve grid congestion through better utilization of distributed energy resources.

More projects — tools, datasets, and experiments — are developed as part of this research and related work.
